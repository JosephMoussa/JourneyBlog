Food Log

Keep track of everything you eat with a simple submission form.

  • Things You Can Include: Show trends, search, filter by date, database of known foods, calorie counting

Recommendation system

Deal finder

Google Map

Keep track of everything you eat with a simple submission form.

Twitter Bot

Weather App

Using API to access infromation to display the weather near you.

Rubik's Cube solver

Creating An App

Publishing an app on the appstore is most luring to recruiters
